What is WWHO?

Previous Next

Where is it? Who has it? And how much?

WWHO is an upcoming sharing economy mobile app for Android and iPhone through which users can loan and lend items to one another. The final release of the app will include options for repeatedly renting oneâ€™s goods to others, and for finding goods from others.

For example, if a user were to throw a family party and they needed a few extra outdoor chairs, they could use the app to find another user willing to rent out their chairs for the day or weekend.

Our service aims to eliminate the inconvenience and wastefulness of purchasing an item at full retail value that will only be used once, as well as to facilitate neighborly, person-to-person interactions.

Frequently Asked Questions

How can I trust my items are returned in working condition?

We evaluate all items that are listed with WWHO and, if the item is returned damaged, are able to charge the borrower the amount of the item.

How do you calculate fees?

We collect a small percentage of the amount an item is rented out for.

What kinds of payment options do you accept?

We accept all major credit cards, as well as paypal.

Where are you in the development process?

We're working on the Android and iPhone front-ends now. We expect to begin beta testing around 2019.
